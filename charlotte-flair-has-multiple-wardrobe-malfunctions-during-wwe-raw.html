Charlotte Flair Has Multiple Wardrobe Malfunctions During WWE RAW

Charlotte Flair made headlines during WWE WrestleMania Backlash with her Cruella inspired getup. The next night on Raw she gained a lot of attention for an entirely different reason. The Queen wrestled Asuka, and she came up short. Flair also had some issues with her wardrobe.

Click here for our complete coverage of WWE Raw from this week.

Charlotte Flair and Asuka had an intense match this week on Raw. While Asuka and Charlotte Flair put in work, The Queen had a little trouble staying in her top.

WWE had to black out the screen multiple times during that match, and fans took notice. This isn’t the first time that Charlotte Flair has experienced issues like this, but it happened several times in one match tonight.

Luckily for Charlotte Flair, they were quick with the censor button in the production truck. No actual slippage made it to the live broadcast, but that doesn’t mean it wasn’t a close call.

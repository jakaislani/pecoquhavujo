Bryan Danielson Match Added To AEW Dynamite Tonight

Bryan Danielson’s accomplishments are well-known in the realm of professional wrestling. Danielson has competed against many opponents in fantastic matches all across the world. It seems he is now up against Wheeler Yuta for tonight’s episode of AEW Dynamite.

A representative of Ring of Honor’s present will go off against one of the promotion’s heroes on tonight’s episode of AEW Dynamite. Wheeler Yuta will square off against Bryan Danielson tonight, two days before Yuta challenged for the ROH Pure Championship at ROH Supercard of Honor, according to AEW President Tony Khan.

Ahead of @WheelerYuta challenging for the ROH Pure Championship at Supercard, he’ll go 1-on-1 against former Pure Champion @bryandanielson on Dynamite TONIGHT! Wheeler Yuta vs. Bryan Danielson + @RealKingRegal on commentary! #AEWDynamite @ 8pm ET/7pm CT on @TBSNetwork TONIGHT!

The bout’s commentary will be provided by William Regal. After Danielson and Jon Moxley defeated Yuta and Chuck Taylor earlier this month, Regal slapped Yuta, who returned to the ring and appeared to hint his interest in joining the group. This singles match may provide him with an opportunity to impress The Blackpool Combat Club, a group of three advocates of violence.

Check out the updated for AEW Dynamite below.

  • Jon Moxley vs. Jay Lethal
  • Wheeler Yuta vs. Bryan Danielson
  • FTR vs. Gunn Club
  • Darby Allin vs. Andrade El Idolo
  • Owen Hart Cup Women’s Qualifier: The Bunny vs. TBA (mystery signing who will sign with AEW)

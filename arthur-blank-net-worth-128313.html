Arthur Blank Net Worth 2024

Arthur Blank is an American businessman and philanthropist born in New York City in 1942. He is best known as the co-founder of the Home Depot hardware and home improvement company, and as the owner of the Atlanta Falcons football team. He has been a major force in the business world and in the sports world, and has made a significant impact on both.

About

Founder of the Home Depot hardware and Home Improvement company. He bought the Atlanta Falcons football team.

Before Fame

He was hired as a senior accountant after graduating college by Arthur Young and Company where he acquired management experience. He opened Home Depot in 1978.

Trivia

He was in talks to buy the Atlanta Braves from Time Warner and to create an expansion MLS team in the city.

Family Life

He had six children with his wife, whom he divorced.

Associated With

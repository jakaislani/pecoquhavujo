Cutter Watson Portales Obituary: In loving memory of Cutter Watson

Cutter Watson Motorcycle Accident – Obituary: In loving memory of Cutter Watson.

Cutter Watson of Portales, New Mexico, died tragically as a result of injuries sustained in a road traffic collision involving a motorcycle, an online obituary announced on Sunday, August 27.

The details concerning the circumstances surrounding the crash are yet to be released, as investigations continue.

Location Guide

Portales is a city in and the county seat of Roosevelt County, New Mexico, United States. Its population was 12,280 at the 2010 census. Portales is located near the larger city of Clovis, and Cannon Air Force Base, a major contributor to the economy of the region.

Meesha Shafi files Rs2bn defamation suit against Ali Zafar

published on September 05, 2024
A well-known Pakistani Singer Meesha Shafi has filed Rs2 billion defamation suit in a Lahore sessions court against Pakistani Singer Ali Zafar for making false claims against her on media, it rose on Wednesday. The defamation suit incorporates Rs1bn for the loss of generosity and notoriety caused, and Rs1bn for “mental torment and misery”. Notwithstanding the damages mentioned, Meesha Shafi requested that the court pass a declaration to announce Zafar’s statements seeing her as “false, vindictive and slanderous” and “made to harm the notoriety of the offended party”.

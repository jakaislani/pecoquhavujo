Answer For structures that don’t need extensive insulation, a layer of aluminium foil insulation or rigid foam may be sandwiched between the finish ceiling and the sheathing to provide enough protection from the elements. Place the sheathing layer on top of the insulation and fasten it using nails that are driven through the insulation and into the rafters of the structure.

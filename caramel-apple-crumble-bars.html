Caramel Apple Crumble Bars

Oozing with caramel goodness, and bursting with sweet apple flavor, these easy Caramel Apple Crumble Bars are the perfect way to celebrate the season!

Course Dessert Cuisine American Keyword caramel apple crumble bars Prep Time 10 minutes minutes Cook Time 23 minutes minutes Total Time 33 minutes minutes Servings 18 bars Calories 274kcal Author Trish - Mom On Timeout

Ingredients

  • 15.25 oz yellow cake mix 1 package
  • 2 1/2 cups quick-cooking oats
  • 3/4 cup butter melted
  • 20 oz apple pie filling 1 can
  • 30 Kraft caramel squares
  • 1 tbsp milk

Instructions

  • Preheat oven to 375 degrees.

  • Line a 9 x 13 baking dish with parchment paper or grease with cooking spray.

  • Mix cake mix and oats in a large bowl.

  • Add melted butter, stir until crumbly.

  • Press down half the crumbs onto the bottom of the pan.

  • Pour apple pie filling into a bowl and use a knife to cut the apple pieces down to bite size.

  • Carefully spread apple pie filling over the crumb crust.

  • Melt caramels with milk in a microwave-safe dish on high heat for 30 seconds. Stir and repeat until caramel is melted.

  • Drizzle caramel over top of apple filling.

  • Spread remaining crumbs over caramel and press down gently.

  • Bake for 18 to 23 minutes or until very lightly golden brown.

  • Eat warm or cool, cut into bars, and serve.
